Admission Procedure
step 1

Enquiry

Parents can visit the school campus or website to collect information about courses, facilities, and admission guidelines.

step 2

Application Form

Fill out the admission form online or offline with accurate student and parent details.

step 3

Entrance / Interaction

Students may attend an entrance test or interaction session to assess their academic level.

step 4

Document Verification

Submit required documents such as birth certificate, previous records, and ID proof for verification.

step 5

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Pay the admission fees to confirm the seat and receive the admission confirmation receipt.
